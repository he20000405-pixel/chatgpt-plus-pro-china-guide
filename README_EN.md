# ChatGPT Plus and Pro Recharge in China

This is the English service guide for [chonggrok.com](https://chonggrok.com/chatgpt), an independent service that helps users pay for a ChatGPT membership subscription on their own account when international card payment is inconvenient.

ChongGrok currently lists three ChatGPT service options: **ChatGPT Plus**, **ChatGPT Pro 100 USD**, and **ChatGPT Pro 200 USD**. Prices in local currency are not fixed in this guide; always check the [live ChatGPT service page](https://chonggrok.com/chatgpt).

> ChongGrok is not affiliated with, authorized by, or officially partnered with OpenAI. ChatGPT plans, models, limits, and official prices are controlled by OpenAI and may change.

## Automated ChatGPT Plus recharge flow

1. Open <https://chonggrok.com/chatgpt>.
2. Select the ChatGPT Plus option.
3. Pay with Alipay or WeChat Pay and keep the card key and order information.
4. Return to <https://chonggrok.com/verify>.
5. Submit the card key to validate the order.
6. Submit the required ChatGPT session credential. Do not submit a password, email code, or recovery code.
7. Verify that the detected account is your own account, then confirm the recharge.
8. After the membership appears, check the plan on ChatGPT, sign out, and sign back in to refresh the previous session.

The public repository intentionally does not provide detailed session-extraction instructions. Necessary guidance belongs inside the controlled flow after card-key verification. A session is sensitive even though it is not a password, and no online process is risk-free.

## ChatGPT Pro 100 USD and Pro 200 USD flow

The two Pro options do not use the automated Plus workflow.

1. Review the Pro options on the [ChatGPT service page](https://chonggrok.com/chatgpt).
2. Contact customer service through the page before paying.
3. Confirm the account status, exact delivery terms, current price, and verification method.
4. After confirmation, customer service uses an overseas credit card to assist with the ChatGPT subscription payment.
5. Verify the resulting membership status, expiration date, and model picker on ChatGPT.

OpenAI introduced ChatGPT Pro as a 200 USD monthly plan in its official announcement. The labels “Pro 100 USD” and “Pro 200 USD” above are ChongGrok service option names; users should not infer an OpenAI plan definition from those labels.

## Payment errors before using a recharge service

For `Your card has been declined` or payment-authentication errors, OpenAI recommends checking the card details, expiration date, CVC, billing address, available funds, issuing-bank restrictions, supported regions, and 3D Secure / Strong Customer Authentication. Clearing browser cookies, using another browser, or contacting the bank may also help.

If you have a supported international card, direct official payment remains an option. If card region, billing address, or 3DS makes direct payment impractical, ChongGrok provides an Alipay or WeChat Pay route for a membership subscription on your own account.

## Scope and safety boundaries

- Membership subscription recharge only; no ChatGPT API credits.
- No ready-made or shared accounts.
- No SMS verification or bulk-registration services.
- No ChatGPT password, email password, verification code, or recovery code is requested.
- A session credential is sensitive and should only be submitted through the verified flow.
- No promise of zero risk, guaranteed account status, or guaranteed model availability.
